The Likivon Legacy

Not just a software startup — a movement to digitize East Africa, one system at a time.

Born in the heart of Dar es Salaam, Likivon Systems was founded by Hemedi Mwarami — combining deep academic training in Software Engineering with a hands-on, industry-first approach to building technology.

We started with a simple observation: too many businesses and public services in Tanzania run on manual processes, spreadsheets, and paper trails that break down as they grow. We build the systems that replace that fragility with something reliable.

Today, we design and ship enterprise systems, mobile applications, AI-powered tools, and secure infrastructure for clients who need software that actually works under real-world pressure.
